Man of Virtue

I want to be a man full of virtue,
Among the legends i want to earn a statue,
The man every child want to be like,
With love and kindness I strike.

I don't want fame,
But for their happiness am to blame,
Not only to be loved by all,
But when in conflict they call.

I don't want to be perfect,
But goodness i want to reflect
I would love the hating,
Inorder to reduce my faulting.

Let them call me the man with virtue,
With my sleek moves they create a character,
For their inspiration to cater,
Yes I will be their mentor.

Trust the Process

There is that one pressure
I was used to feel as long as I could breathe
With it come darkness that blocked the light
That was considered as a basic to some but
I didn't share the privilege.

It kept my physical down the sole
Always being trampled on for their pleasure
It was always by luck that I was not crushed
Into broken unrecognizable fragments of being
Their high ground choked out any sign of confidence.

My escape from this cloud that was blunt to my efforts,
What if it just illusion? What if my limitations caught up with my strength?
Afraid you can call it, it was all fear and confusion
On how to stand up and raise my fly high,
Then the pain was enough to fuel my rage towards freedom.

Slowly their stand weakened due to persist of my impact
'Till I got strong hold that shoved their strength and shifting their positions.
Suited for battle and it repaired my weakened confidence, got the freedom yearned for and got the privilege to be my own man.

© hushdarlin.
